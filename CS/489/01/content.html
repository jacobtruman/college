Problem 1

Parsing Records of TIGR's osa1 Database, Version 4

It's better if you do this problem yourself so you understand how things break up. Example below.

By Hand (no program), take Os08g17400 (chromosome 8, gene 17400) and:

  1. With Os08g17400 of chr08.1kUpstream
    1. Locate the 5' UTR in Os08g17400 of chr08.1kUpstream.
    2. Locate the nearest 'tata' before the 5' UTR in Os08g17400 of chr08.1kUpstream.
  2. With Os08g17400 of chr08.seq
    1. Mark off Os08g17400 of chr08.seq up into exon 1, intron 1, exon 2, intron 2, exon 3, intron 3, and exon 4.
    2. Also mark off 5' UTR at the beginning and 3' UTR at the end of Os08g17400 of chr08.seq.
  3. With Os08g17400 of chr08.cDNA
    1. Mark off exon 1, exon 2, exon 3, exon 4 in Os08g17400 of chr08.cDNA.
    2. Mark off 5' UTR at the beginning and 3' UTR at the end of Os08g17400 of chr08.cDNA.

Notes:

  1. cDNA = 5'UTR + cds + 3'UTR.
  2. seq = cDNA with the introns interspersed.
